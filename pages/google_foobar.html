Google offered me a CHALLENGE

Foobar Challenge

In 2015, users of the Google search engine stumbled upon a secret surprise called the Foobar Challenge. It was like a hidden gem, a cleverly disguised coding challenge that gradually escalated in difficulty. Whenever someone searched for certain programming-related topics, they were greeted with a search results page containing an intriguing invitation to take part in the challenge. The challenge itself was divided into six levels, each demanding the user to crack a programming puzzle or solve an algorithmic problem. Those who managed to conquer all six levels were rewarded with a warm congratulatory message and an opportunity to share their contact details with Google. Although the specific intricacies and specifics of the challenge are not openly disclosed, its purpose was clear: to assess and evaluate participant's programming abilities and knack for problem-solving.
